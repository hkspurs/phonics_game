# Phonics Learning Platform — Sound Family List

The Sound Mastery Map visualizes the child's progress through these distinct regions.

## Region 1: Vowel Beach
**Focus**: Basic A, E, I, O, U combinations ending in 'B'.
- AB (cab)
- EB (web)
- IB (rib)
- OB (cob)
- UB (cub)

## Region 2: Sound Forest
**Focus**: Vowel combinations ending in 'G'.
- AG (bag)
- EG (peg)
- IG (pig)
- OG (dog)
- UG (bug)

## Region 3: Echo Cave
**Focus**: Vowel combinations ending in 'X'.
- AX (wax)
- EX (Rex)
- IX (six)
- OX (box)
- UX (tux)

## Region 4: Rocket Valley
**Focus**: Vowel combinations ending in 'T'.
- AT (cat)
- ET (vet)
- IT (sit)
- OT (hot)
- UT (hut)

## Map Node Status Indicators
Each sound family node on the map will display its current mastery status:
- **Grey**: Not started
- **Blue**: Practising (Heard before)
- **Green**: Strong / Improving
- **Gold**: Mastered
- **Red Mark**: Weak sound (needs review)
- **Purple Mark**: Developer only (Audio human-reviewed and confirmed)

## Confusable Pairs (For the "Comparison" Question Template)
- AB vs EB
- IG vs EG
- AX vs EX
- IX vs EX
- AT vs IT
- OT vs UT
